Who We Are

Jindagi Ek Shuruaat is a community-driven NGO dedicated to helping the underprivileged and creating a better society.

We focus on providing food, clothing, and essential support to those in need while also working towards animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social awareness.

Our mission is simple — to serve humanity with compassion and create real impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To help the needy, protect animals, and spread awareness in society.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and sustainable world where everyone lives with dignity.

Our Values

Compassion, Service, Humanity, and Responsibility.
