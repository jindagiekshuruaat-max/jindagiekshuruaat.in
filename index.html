Who We Are

Born from empathy,
driven by purpose

Our organization is committed to uplifting lives through food distribution, clothing support, animal care, and social awareness campaigns focused on girls' safety and child labour. We also actively contribute to environmental well-being through plantation initiatives.

With collective effort, we aim to build a safer and more responsible society. Be a part of our journey.

🍱

Food & Clothing Support

Distributing meals and clothing to the hungry and underprivileged with warmth and dignity.

🛡️

Social Awareness Campaigns

🌿

Environment & Animal Care

Plantation initiatives and rescue care for stray animals — compassion for all living beings.

What We Do

Six programs, one mission

From feeding the hungry to planting trees, from protecting girls to caring for animals — every program is a step towards a kinder society.

🍱

Food Distribution

We regularly distribute cooked meals and dry rations to the homeless, daily-wage workers, and underprivileged families — ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

Learn more →
👕

Clothing Support

Collecting and distributing clothes to those in need, especially during winters — providing warmth and restoring dignity to every recipient.

Learn more →
🛡️

Girls' Safety Awareness

Conducting awareness campaigns in schools and communities to educate girls about their rights, personal safety, and avenues for help.

Learn more →
🧒

Child Labour Prevention

Raising awareness about child labour laws, working with families to keep children in school, and advocating for every child's right to a childhood.

Learn more →
🌿

Plantation Initiatives

Organising tree plantation drives across Nautanwa, Maharajganj and surrounding areas to improve air quality and restore green cover.

Learn more →
🐾

Animal Care

Feeding, rescuing, and providing basic medical aid to stray and injured animals — compassion for all living beings lies at the heart of our work.

Learn more →

Founder's Desk

A message from our founder

Upload Photo Here
Ashutosh Singh Rajpoot
Founder, Jindagi Ek Shuruaat
"

Every meaningful change begins with a simple idea. When I founded this organization, my vision was clear — to create a society where every individual has access to dignity, opportunity, and support.

We believe that true strength lies in compassion, unity, and collective action. Our mission is not just to provide help, but to empower people so they can transform their own lives and build a better future.

This journey has not always been easy, but every smile, every small victory, and every life we touch gives us the courage to keep moving forward.

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to all our volunteers, donors, and supporters who have stood with us and believed in this mission.

Together, let us continue to create a positive and lasting impact.

Ashutosh Singh Rajpoot
Founder
