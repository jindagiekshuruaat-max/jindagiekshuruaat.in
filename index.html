About Us

Jindagi Ek Shuruaat

Creating impact through compassion and action.

Focused on community welfare, animal care, and social awareness, we are dedicated to addressing critical issues such as hunger, safety, child labour, and environmental sustainability.

Together, we can create meaningful change.

Our Work

Food Distribution Campaign

We organize regular food distribution drives in slum areas and roadside locations where people struggle for daily meals. Our team prepares and distributes nutritious food packets to ensure no one sleeps hungry. This campaign focuses on dignity, care, and immediate hunger relief.

Blanket Distribution Campaign

During harsh winters, we distribute blankets and warm clothes to homeless and underprivileged people. Many families suffer due to extreme cold, and this initiative helps them survive safely and comfortably during winter nights.

Meal Distribution Campaign

We provide freshly cooked meals to people in need across different areas. This campaign ensures hygiene, quality food, and timely distribution, especially targeting daily wage workers and needy families.

Animal Rescue & Welfare

We actively rescue injured and abandoned animals, provide them medical treatment, shelter, and care. Our mission is to protect voiceless lives and promote kindness towards animals in society.

Plantation Drive

We conduct plantation drives to promote environmental sustainability. By planting trees and spreading awareness, we aim to create a greener and healthier future for upcoming generations.

Women Safety Campaign

We organize awareness campaigns for women safety, educating girls about their rights, self-defense, and safety measures. Our goal is to empower women and build a safer society.
