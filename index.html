🍱 Food Distribution

We regularly distribute cooked meals and dry rations to the homeless, daily-wage workers, and underprivileged families — ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

About This Program

Our food distribution program operates every week, reaching daily-wage workers, homeless individuals, and underprivileged families across Nautanwa and Maharajganj. We distribute freshly cooked meals as well as dry ration kits including rice, dal, oil, and flour.

What We Do

  • Weekly cooked meal distribution at identified locations
  • Monthly dry ration kits for families below poverty line
  • Special food camps during festivals and emergencies
  • Coordination with local volunteers and donors for large drives

Our Impact So Far

Over 500 meals distributed every month, reaching more than 200 families with ration support annually.

How to Get Involved

You can volunteer to cook, pack, or distribute food. Donations of Rs 500 feed a family for a week.

👕 Clothing Support

Collecting and distributing clothes to those in need, especially during winters — providing warmth and restoring dignity to every recipient.

About This Program

Our clothing support program collects gently used and new clothes from donors and distributes them to those in need — with a special focus on winter months when the need is most urgent.

What We Do

  • Year-round clothing collection drives in schools, offices, and communities
  • Special winter blanket and warm clothing distribution
  • Targeted support for children, elderly, and daily wage workers
  • Organized distribution camps in underserved localities

Our Impact So Far

Over 2,000 pieces of clothing and 300+ blankets distributed annually.

How to Get Involved

Donate clothes, blankets, or funds. Volunteer at collection and distribution drives.

🛡️ Girls Safety Awareness

Conducting awareness campaigns in schools and communities to educate girls about their rights, personal safety, and avenues for help.

About This Program

This program focuses on empowering girls and women with knowledge about personal safety, legal rights, and support systems. We conduct workshops, seminars, and community discussions to break silence around harassment, abuse, and gender inequality.

What We Do

  • Safety awareness workshops in schools and colleges
  • Street plays and nukkad nataks on girls rights and safety
  • Training on self-defense basics for young girls
  • Helpline awareness drives spreading knowledge about emergency numbers
  • Engagement with parents and community leaders

Our Impact So Far

Over 1,500 girls reached through workshops. Campaigns conducted in 15+ schools.

How to Get Involved

Volunteer as a trainer, counsellor, or awareness speaker. Support us in reaching more schools.

🧒 Child Labour Prevention

Raising awareness about child labour laws, working with families to keep children in school, advocating for every child's right to a proper childhood.

About This Program

Child labour robs children of their childhood and future. Our program identifies families at risk, raises awareness about child labour laws, and connects families with government schemes for financial support.

What We Do

  • Community awareness campaigns on child labour laws
  • Identification and reporting of child labour instances
  • Connecting families with government welfare schemes and scholarships
  • School enrollment drives to bring out-of-school children back to education
  • Coordination with district administration and local NGOs

Our Impact So Far

Helped 100+ children leave labour and re-enroll in school. Awareness reached 50+ villages.

How to Get Involved

Help us identify cases, spread awareness, or sponsor a child's education.

🌿 Plantation Initiatives

Organising tree plantation drives across Nautanwa, Maharajganj and surrounding areas to improve air quality and restore green cover.

About This Program

Our plantation initiative brings together volunteers, schools, and communities to plant and nurture trees across Nautanwa and Maharajganj. We focus on native species that support local biodiversity.

What We Do

  • Mass plantation drives on World Environment Day and Van Mahotsav
  • School plantation programs with awareness sessions
  • Maintenance visits to planted saplings for 6 months post-planting
  • Distribution of saplings to households for home planting

Our Impact So Far

1,000+ trees planted across 20+ locations. Active partnerships with 10+ schools.

How to Get Involved

Join a plantation drive, sponsor saplings, or adopt a tree and track its growth.

🐾 Animal Care

Feeding, rescuing, and providing basic medical aid to stray and injured animals — because a compassionate society extends its care to all living beings.

About This Program

Our animal care program addresses the suffering of stray and injured animals. We run regular feeding routes, rescue injured animals, provide first aid, and coordinate with local veterinarians for treatment.

What We Do

  • Daily feeding routes for stray dogs and cats in identified areas
  • Rescue and first aid for injured or sick stray animals
  • Coordination with local vets for medical treatment and vaccination drives
  • Awareness campaigns on animal rights and responsible pet ownership
  • Anti-cruelty reporting support

Our Impact So Far

10+ animals rescued and treated. Regular feeding at 8 locations across Nautanwa.

How to Get Involved

Volunteer on feeding routes, support with vet costs, or report animals in need.
